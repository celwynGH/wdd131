Camiguin Island is an island in Northern Mindanao Philippines, also known as Island Born of Fire. It is a volcanic island in the Philippines known for its dramatic landscapes, natural beauty, and unique attractions. It's a popular destination for those seeking a mix of adventure, relaxation, and cultural experiences.
